Never finished

Never Finish by gmcmullen

  1. Many times I’ve wished Bloop was finished: I’d like to start the next project.
  2. Bloop will never be finished.
  3. Feedback from users, investors, mentors.
  4. No two people ever want exactly the same thing (compromise is obviously the key).
  5. Agencies are contracted to complete a project, and paid on completion.
  6. Startups are rewarded for execution of an idea, and risk on exit.
  7. Websites are never finished either.
  8. It ages, and is rebuilt by the same, or another agency in a few years time.

The tools you know

iLiKETRAiNS 01 by samsaunders

  1. Use the tools you know. Don’t be attracted to exotic tools (yet).
  2. Bloop uses: jQuery, Python, Django, PostgreSQL, Ubuntu, memcached.
  3. All things I knew before I started working on Bloop.
  4. We both use our own Macs: we are (and most Mac users I know) are way more productive on their Mac than they would be on a Windows 7 Dell.
  5. Bloop doesn’t use:
  6. mongodb: we don’t have a huge need for non-relational storage (yet). Point also helped by the Foursquare, 11-hour outage the week we discussed it.
  7. Node.js: I’ve never managed to keep it up, it’s fun to write JavaScript for the server, and to do real–time stuff, but we don’t need real–time (yet).
